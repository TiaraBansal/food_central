Lemony Arugula Salad with Couscous, Cucumbers, and Feta


This salad is a delightful mix of textures and flavors. The lemony feta combination is a classic and delicious pairing, while the couscous adds heartiness without being heavy. The refreshing crunch of cucumbers adds another layer of texture and coolness. You can easily substitute orzo pasta, quinoa, or wheat berries for the couscous if you prefer!

Ingredients :

Cooked Couscous

Baby Arugula

Chopped Cucumber

Feta Cheese

Lemon Juice

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Honey

Dried basil/freshly chopped basil)

Salt

Pepper

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Instruction:

You'll start by whisking together lemon juice, olive oil, honey, basil, salt, and pepper.

Toss in the cooked and let it sit for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld.

Once the couscous has cooled slightly, add the arugula, cucumbers, and feta.

Toss everything together well and enjoy the refreshing and satisfying flavors of this simple yet delicious salad!
